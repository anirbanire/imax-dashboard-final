IMAX Post-COVID Recovery Dashboard

This dashboard analyses how IMAX has recovered after the COVID‑19 shock compared with cinema peers Cinemark (CNK) and AMC, and how its financial performance and IMAX box office track against the wider global box office market from 2020–2025F.

IMAX Share Price (Timeline)

  • IMAX trades above 20 USD at the start of 2020 but sells off sharply as COVID‑19 hits, reaching single‑digit levels in March.
  • The price then stabilises and begins to grind higher, setting the context for later comparison with CNK and AMC.

IMAX vs CNK vs AMC Share Prices

  • IMAX trades more steadily than AMC, which experiences sharper spikes and drops during the COVID‑19 period.
  • By late 2020, IMAX and CNK prices stabilise while AMC remains more volatile at lower absolute levels.

Post‑COVID Stock Recovery (Indexed to 100)

  • All three cinema stocks fall sharply below the 100 baseline in early 2020, with AMC suffering the deepest drawdown.
  • IMAX and CNK recover closer to their pre‑COVID baseline, while AMC’s indexed value stays more depressed overall.

IMAX Revenue and Net Income (2020–2025F)

  • Revenue nearly doubles from 2020 to 2021 and continues to grow through 2023, with a forecasted step‑up again in 2025F.
  • Net income turns from deep losses in 2020 to positive territory after 2022, supporting a more sustainable recovery profile.

Global vs IMAX Box Office (USD billions)

  • Global box office recovers from 20 billion in 2020 to a peak of 33 billion by 2023 before a slight dip in 2024.
  • IMAX box office rebounds faster in relative terms, rising from 0.1 to over 1.0 billion, signalling strong demand for premium formats.

Key recommendations for IMAX

  • Continue investing in premium large‑format releases and exclusive windows, as IMAX box office recovers faster than the wider market.
  • Leverage the stronger balance sheet and more stable share price relative to AMC to negotiate favourable terms with studios and exhibitors.
  • Prioritise growth in markets where post‑COVID cinema recovery and IMAX index performance are strongest, while managing exposure to weaker regions.
